Butter Chicken Recipes

Butter chicken is soooo goood, especially the one my mom makes. I dont know about this recipe though.

The prep time for this recipe is 15 mins and the cook time is 4 hr and 15 mins (this is a slow cooker recipes) resulting in a total of 4 hrs and 30 mins

This recipe makes 6 servings.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 4 large skinless, boneless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon curry paste
  • 2 teaspoons curry powder
  • 2 teaspoons tandoori masala
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 15 green cardamom pods
  • 1 (14 ounce) can coconut milk
  • 1 cup low-fat plain yogurt
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Warm butter and vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Stir in chicken, onion, and garlic. Cook and stir until onion has softened and turned transclucent, about 10 minutes. Stir in tomato paste, curry paste, curry powder, tandoori masala, and garam masala until no lumps of tomato paste remain.
  2. Pour mixture into a slow cooker; stir in cardamom pods, coconut milk, and yogurt. Season with salt.
  3. Cook on high for 4-6 hours (or on low for 6-8 hours) until chicken is tender and sauce has reduced to desired consistency. Remove and discard cardamom pods (seriously remember to remove these because they really suck and it will ruin to mood when you get one of them in your bite by accident) before serving.
