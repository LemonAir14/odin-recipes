Cheesecake Recipe

Cheesecakes are great!

The prep time for this recipe is 30 mins, and the cook time is 1 hr and 10 mins resulting in a total time of 1 hr and 40 mins

With this recipe you can make a 9 inch cheesecake.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped walnuts
  • 3 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 5 tablespoons butter,melted
  • 3 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 3 tabelspoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 3 eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 C). In a medium bowl, combine graham cracker crumbs, chopped walnuts, brown sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg and melted butter. Mix well and press in to the bottom of a 9 inch sprinform pan. Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes. Remove from oven and allow to cool
  2. In a large bowl, beat cream cheese and sugar together until smooth. Beat in sour cream and heavy cream. Blend in the flour and vanilla. With mixer on low speed, add eggs one at a time. Pour batter over crust.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven fro 60-70 minutes, or until center is almost set (filling will be soft). Refrigerate at least 6 hours overnight before removing from pan.
